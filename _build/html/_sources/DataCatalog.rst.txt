.. _data-catalog-header-three:

Data Catalog Header Three
=========================

To make your numbered list, follow these steps:

#. Thing 1
#. Thing 2
#. Thing 3

Here's how to include a tip, which appears in a green box:

.. tip::
	This is the tip text.

Here's how to include a note, which appears in a blue box:

.. note::
	This is the note text.

Here's how to tell Ian to make an icon or a GIF for your page: put its name |example_icon| between two pipes where you want the icon or GIF to appear (or make it yourself and add it to the globals.txt file).

|example_gif|
